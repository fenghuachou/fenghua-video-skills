# Scriptwriting Guidelines for Short Explainer Videos

## Core Principle: Write for the EAR, not the EYE

The script will be read aloud by TTS. Every sentence must sound natural when spoken.

## Section-by-Section Guide

### 1. Hook (5-10s)

**Goal:** Stop the scroll. The viewer decides in 3 seconds whether to keep watching.

**Techniques:**
- **Question hook**: "你有没有想过，为什么有些人能让 100 个 AI 同时为他工作？"
- **Surprising fact**: "一个人调度 100 个 Agent 的产出，可能超过 1000 个不用 AI 的人。"
- **Bold claim**: "AI 时代，最危险的不是被 AI 取代，而是你还在当 AI 的保姆。"
- **Counter-intuitive**: "让 AI 犯错，可能是提升效率最快的方法。"

**Avoid:**
- Starting with "大家好" or self-introduction
- Starting with background/context (too slow)
- Vague openings: "今天我们聊一聊..."

### 2. Pain Point (10-20s)

**Goal:** Make the viewer feel "this is about ME."

**Template:**
```
[Relatable scenario] + [Why it matters] + [Implied promise]
```

**Example:**
"你是不是每次让 AI 干活，都要盯着它？它做一步，你确认一步，就像个监工。
结果你自己比亲手做还累。问题不在 AI 不够聪明——而在于你的管理方式还停留在手把手带实习生的阶段。"

### 3. Arguments (60-120s)

**Goal:** Deliver 2-3 core points, each with evidence.

**Per-point structure:**
```
[Claim in 1 sentence]
[Evidence: example, data, or analogy]
[So-what: why this matters to the viewer]
```

**Transition words between points:**
- "第一个关键是..." / "更重要的是..." / "但最颠覆的一点是..."
- Avoid: "第一点、第二点、第三点"（too mechanical）

**Duration rule:** Each point should take 20-40 seconds. If a point takes more than 40s, split it.

### 4. Twist (10-20s)

**Goal:** The "aha moment" — reframe, surprising quote, or counter-argument.

**Techniques:**
- **Key quote**: "余一说了一句话让我印象深刻：'如果我不想介入，那我就应该对结果妥协。'"
- **Reframe**: "所以本质上，这不是一个技术问题，而是一个管理问题。"
- **Prediction**: "按这个趋势，明年你的竞争对手可能不是另一个人，而是另一个人的 100 个 Agent。"

### 5. Closing (5-15s)

**Goal:** Leave a lasting impression and prompt action.

**Techniques:**
- **Call to action**: "从今天起，试着给 AI 一个完整的任务，然后——不要盯着它。"
- **Thought question**: "想想看，如果你有 100 个不知疲倦的 AI 员工，你会先让他们做什么？"
- **Callback to hook**: Echo the opening question/statement with the answer implied.

## Language Rules

| Rule | Good | Bad |
|------|------|-----|
| Short sentences | "AI 不是工具。它是员工。" | "AI 不仅仅是一个工具，它更像是一个可以自主完成任务的员工。" |
| Active voice | "余一让 AI 自己写规则。" | "规则是由 AI 自己编写的。" |
| Concrete > Abstract | "40 分钟，从零到成品视频。" | "效率得到了显著的提升。" |
| Numbers > Vague | "20 张图，只有 1 张没过品控。" | "品控通过率非常高。" |
| Conversational | "说白了，就是..." | "简而言之，其核心要义在于..." |

## Duration Estimation

- Chinese: ~250 characters/minute (including pauses)
- English: ~150 words/minute
- Add 10% buffer for natural pauses

## Title Writing Rules

**Formula: [Target audience signal] + [Curiosity gap] + [Concrete outcome]**

**Scoring checklist (each title must pass ALL):**
1. Target audience recognizes "this is for me" within 1 second
2. Creates CURIOSITY (I roughly know what this is about, want to know more) not CONFUSION (I have no idea what this means)
3. Under 20 Chinese characters / 10 English words
4. No jargon that the audience wouldn't know

**Compare:**
- "棉手套效应与AI协作" → Confusion (what is cotton glove effect?)
- "别再当AI的保姆了" → Curiosity (I do babysit my AI... what should I do instead?)
